The colorclock was a homework assignment at The Iron Yard. The assignment was to build a live clock that also changes the color of the background of the website using Javascript. After completing the Javscript portion I wanted to add some style to the clock. I always was fascinated by designers making everyday objects out of just CSS. For this project I decided to attempt making an iPhone using just CSS. View my colorclock live here.
